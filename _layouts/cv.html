• In October 2016 I'll start working as a postdoctoral researcher at the Geography Department of the University of Bonn, in the research group of Prof. Julia Verne.

• I worked as a digital anthropologist in the ChemicalYouth group at the Anthropology Deparment of the University of Amsterdam from 2015 to 2016.

• Between 2011 and 2013, I worked as an intern and graduate research assistant: in trend research at GIM market research Heidelberg, in innovation research and foresight at the Fraunhofer Institute for System- and Innovation Research, and at the Institute for Innovation Studies, Utrecht University.

• For social media analyses, I regularly work with DMI tools such as Netvizz, and with Gephi for network analyses.

• I use Python for data collection and scraping (BeautifulSoup, Pattern), for automated text analyses (Pattern, NLTK), and for building networks (networkx) and simple graphs (matplotlib).

• German is my mother tongue.

• My English and Hebrew are almost as fluent.

• My Dutch, French, and Italian can be put to some use.
